About reFern Marketplace

Our Story

reFern was created with the intent to solve modern marketplace problems. We understand how frustrating it can be to wait for answer back from buyers and sellers alike. 

How we 're different

Other marketplaces still expect you to sort through chats for the right buyer and figure out how your item is going to be delivered.


Through a bidding process, reFern allows the buyer to put in offers on items they want, ask questions about the product, and the seller doesn't have to worry about being overwhelmed with a popping inbox. You shouldn't have to suffer because your items are just that good.

Cities and Neighborhoods

We're starting local in Toronto to make sure reFern meets the expectations of buyers and sellers alike. We're planning to expand to other big cities in Canada in the future!
